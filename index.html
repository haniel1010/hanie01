Our Story

Introducing MIH

They say every great design begins with an even better story. At Make It Home, we’ve certainly found this to be true.

Our love for entertaining goes far beyond your first impression. We’ve been designing beautiful, ethically sourced home goods from around the world for over 15 years. Each of our products was crafted by a pair of hands before finding itself in yours. We are obsessed with serving the customer, and as you can see, that passion has brought us to where we are today.
